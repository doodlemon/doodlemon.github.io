Our Story

Food made the
way it always was

The Stone Hearth was born from a simple belief: that the best food comes from hands that care, fires that burn slow, and ingredients grown nearby. Our oak-fired oven reaches 900 degrees — and never cools down.

Every dough is fermented for 48 hours. Every pasta is rolled that morning. Every sauce starts with tomatoes we source from local farms each season. We're not fast. We're not trying to be.
