Our Story

Born from a tiny kitchen,
a whole lot of butter

Sucrée started as Sunday afternoon baking sessions that got completely out of hand. What began as a hobby turned into something we couldn't stop sharing — so we stopped trying to.

Every macaron, tart, and cake is made by hand each morning. We use real butter, seasonal fruit, and zero shortcuts. Come in for a slice. Stay for the whole thing.
